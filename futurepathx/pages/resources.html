✍️ Resume Building Tips
01
Use Action Verbs
Start bullet points with strong verbs: "Developed", "Implemented", "Optimized", "Led".
02
Quantify Results
Use numbers: "Improved model accuracy by 15%", "Reduced API response time by 40%".
03
ATS Optimization
Match keywords from the job description. Avoid graphics, tables, or columns in ATS resumes.
04
One Page Rule
Keep your resume to 1 page if you have under 5 years of experience. Quality over quantity.
05
Tailor for Each Job
Customize your resume for each application. Highlight the most relevant experience and skills.
06
Strong Summary
Write 2–3 sentences about who you are, your top skills, and your career goal.
